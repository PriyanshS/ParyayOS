🏷️ Verification & Registry Flow
1
Metered Data Aggregation
Smart meters and generation inverters log kWh data to the server in 15-min intervals. Tamper-evident via cryptographic hash.
2
Emission Factor Mapping
Platform applies regional grid emission factor (CEA India: 0.716 kgCO₂/kWh) to calculate tCO₂e avoided monthly.
3
Protocol Alignment
Reports auto-formatted to VERRA VCS, Gold Standard, or BEE-India protocol. Scope 1/2/3 classification is automatic.
4
Third-Party Verification Export
One-click export of MRV package for external auditor or registry submission.
5
Credit Issuance & Trading
Upon registry approval, issued CERs are tracked in-platform. Trade, retire, or bank toward institutional net-zero targets.
